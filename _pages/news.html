ICFO-KNUST International School on the Frontiers of Light

Oct 8 - 14, 2023 Kumasi, Ghana

I recently completed a 5-day school on Photonics organized in partnership with the Department of Physics at the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Kumasi, Ghana. The school featured local and international experts and covered a variety of topics, including:

  • Clean Energy
  • Medical Photonics
  • Ultrafast Spectroscopy & Quantum Materials
  • Terahertz Spectroscopy
  • Quantum Sensing
  • Optical Metrology

The event included lectures, discussion sessions, a professional development workshop, student talks and posters, and social activities to promote interaction between students and lecturers.
